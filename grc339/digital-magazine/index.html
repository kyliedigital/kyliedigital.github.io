Fashion In Music

Fashion and music have always moved together, shaping culture in ways that go beyond trends. From the polished glamour of Motown in the 1960s to the rebellious leather jackets of punk rock in the 1970s, artists have used clothing as an extension of their sound and identity.

2025 Fashion Show

Last year’s FITS fashion show, ROOTS, celebrated identity, culture, and the stories that shape who we are. From earthy color palettes and natural textures to bold prints and symbolic accessories, every look on the runway reflected individuality and connection.

2024 Fashion Show

The 2024 FITS fashion show, SYMBIOSIS, explored the beauty of connection and coexistence through fashion. Inspired by the relationships between nature, technology, art, and humanity, the show highlighted how contrasting elements can come together to create something powerful and innovative.
